## About Me

I am currently pursuing a **Master's degree in Computer Science at UNESP (São Paulo State University)**, researching **Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Information Retrieval, Graph Neural Networks, Deep Metric Learning, and Probabilistic Learning**.

My academic background combines **Statistics**, **Data Science**, and **Computer Science**, allowing me to bridge theoretical foundations with practical AI applications.

I am particularly interested in developing methods that improve how machines represent, retrieve, classify, and reason about complex information.
