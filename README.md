# Hi, I'm Luiz Gustavo Bravo 👋

I'm a Master's student in Computer Science at UNESP and hold a Bachelor's degree in Statistics and Data Science from the University of São Paulo (USP).

My work sits at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Statistics, Information Retrieval, Deep Learning, and Network Science. I am particularly interested in how ideas from different fields can be combined to build more robust and interpretable intelligent systems.

Currently, my research focuses on developing probabilistic and contextual learning methods for Information Retrieval and Computer Vision, exploring topics such as Deep Metric Learning, Graph Neural Networks, uncertainty estimation, and ranking optimization.
